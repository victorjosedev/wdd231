About Mountain E-Riders

We are a passionate community of electric mountain bike riders dedicated to exploring the world's most challenging and beautiful trails. Founded in 2020, Mountain E-Riders has grown from a small group of friends to a thriving community of over 500 members across North America.

Our Mission

To empower riders of all skill levels to explore mountain trails safely and sustainably using electric bike technology, fostering a community built on adventure, respect for nature, and shared passion.

What We Do

We organize weekly group rides, maintain a curated database of e-bike-friendly trails, host skills clinics, and advocate for responsible trail access. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a place for you here.

Why E-Bikes?

Electric mountain bikes open up new possibilities: climb higher, ride further, and recover faster. They make the sport more accessible while maintaining the thrill and challenge that mountain bikers love.
